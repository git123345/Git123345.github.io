Atmanirbhar Jammu

"An insight into economic self-sustainance of Jammu Province."

1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8

Motivated by Honourable Prime Minister, Sh. Narendra Modi’s visionary concept of Atmanirbhar Bharat, a 6th class student, Jayendra Singh hailing from village Barwal of Kathua District and studying in Jodhamal Public School has developed and designed a website by the title of “Atmanirbhar Jammu”.

The website has been coded by Jayendra independently using HTML, CSS and JAVA SCRIPT. This website is focused on showing to the world the unique aspects of 10 districts of Jammu Division. It has info-graphics of local products of Kathua, Udhampur, Doda, Bhaderwah, Kishtwar, Reasi, Rajouri and Poonch districts. It provides first hand information to the world about:

  1. Local Basmati Rice
  2. Kishtwari Saffron
  3. Bhaderwahi Rajmash
  4. Udhampur’s Kalari
  5. Basohli Painings and many more……

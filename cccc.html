Kaladi Cheese


Kalari is a traditional ripened cheese product exclusive to the Udhampur district of Jammu province in Jammu and Kashmir UT. Udhampur is a quaint district nestled on the Pir Panjal and Shivalik Ranges in Jammu. Kalari is usually made from cow's or buffalo's milk, though kalaris made from goat's milk are also available and have a whitish color. "Kalari-Kulcha" is a popular snack in the Jammu province. Kalari is also served as Kalari Curry (Kalari ki Sabzi), Kalari Burger, etc. on the menu lists of roadside vendors and food courts. It is one of the less explored cheeses of Jammu Province and is one of the most energetic foods. Since agriculture and cattle rearing are the primary occupations of the locals in these hilly districts, they have mastered the craft of making the best out of the fresh and high-quality products they obtain from their livestock and surroundings. Kalari Cheese is one of the finest examples of how goat and cow milk get used to make products that are consumed by food lovers from across the globe with much fervor. Kalari is also a high-value dairy product that has the potential to raise the per capita income of small dairy farm holders in the hilly districts of Jammu Province. Kalari is prepared from uncooked milk and contains full-fat calories and energy. Milk is dried under the sun for a few days until it gets solidified. The raw or uncooked milk of cows, buffalo, or goats is poured into a small container made of tree leaves known as "doona" and then kept under the sun for a few days so that the outer layer gets solidified whereas moisture remains in the inner layers, which makes Kalari tasty and soft. As the ambient temperature is low and the sun is strong in the mountains, the kalari becomes dry from the outside yet retains moisture from the inside. Kalari is a great source of biotin (Vitamin B7), which is very useful for healthy hair, skin, and nails. Like many kinds of dairy products, Kalari is a great source of calcium, which helps to support bone strength. Kalari cheese is also a great source of potassium, which helps lower blood pressure and can rectify abysmal heart rhythms. Riboflavin, or Vitamin B2, is another vitamin found in Kalari that helps the body fight multiple conditions or ailments, such as migraines and anemia.

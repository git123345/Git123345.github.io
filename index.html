Atmanirbhar Jammu

Motivated by Honourable Prime Minister Sh. Narendra Modi’s visionary concept of Atmanirbhar Bharat which will lead the country to be a gloriously self-reliant developed nation, I, Jayendra Singh Jasrotia, a 6th-class student of Jodhamal Public School, Jammu (Jammu and Kashmir UT), have compiled and reflected the self-reliant image of Jammu province of JK UT of India in a website with the title "Atmanirbhar Jammu".
This website has been coded by me independently using HTML, CSS and JAVA SCRIPT. This website is focused on showing the world the unique aspects of ten districts in the Jammu division. It has infographics of local products from Jammu, Kathua, Udhampur, Doda, Kishtwar, Reasi, Rajouri, Poonch, Samba and Ramban districts which can contribute a lot to the techno-economic development of this province. The salient features of this website consist of an illustrative presentation of local products from Jammu province such as:

  1. 1) Reasi Lithium – an electronic vehicle (EV) game changer for India
  2. 2) Lavender products – a purple revolution of Bhaderwah, Doda
  3. 3). Rajmash (kidney beans) from Bhaderwah, Doda
  4. 4). Saffron-n-Sapphire from Kishtwar
  5. 5). Basmati Rice from R.S. Pura, Jammu
  6. 6). Kalari (cheese) from Udhampur
  7. 7). Paintings from Basohli, Kathua
  8. 8) Wool from Poonch and many more--
This website will popularize our local products and go a long way in ensuring the economic self-reliance of the local population of these districts of Jammu province.
